Publications

  • Hierarchical Incorporation of rGO into Anisotropic CNF Foams

    Journal: ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces, 2024

    Abstract: This study explores the hierarchical integration of reduced graphene oxide (rGO) into cellulose nanofiber (CNF) foams to enhance their mechanical and thermal properties. The research demonstrates significant improvements in foam strength and conductivity, making them suitable for advanced applications in electronics and energy storage. Layer-by-layer self-assembly was utilized for hierarchical material modifications without compromising foam integrity.

    Read More »

  • Moisture-Resilient Aramid/Cellulose Nanofiber Foams

    Journal: Advanced Materials, 2023

    Abstract: This research focuses on the development of thermally insulating and moisture-resilient foams using upcycled aramid nanofibers and nanocellulose. The study demonstrates exceptional thermal conductivity values as low as 18 mW m⁻¹ K⁻¹ across a wide range of relative humidity (20-80% RH), showcasing the potential for sustainable and high-performance insulation materials.

    Read More »

  • Magnetic Hybrid Foams from Fe3O4@TA and TOCN

    Journal: RSC Advances, 2023

    Abstract: This research introduces highly magnetic hybrid foams synthesized using aligned tannic acid-coated iron oxide nanoparticles and TEMPO-oxidized cellulose nanofibers (TOCN). The resulting foams demonstrate excellent magnetic responsiveness and mechanical strength, achieving magnetization values close to 95% of bulk magnetite performance. The innovative magnetic field-assisted ice templating method enhanced both mechanical and thermal properties, making these foams suitable for applications in electromagnetic shielding and environmental remediation.

    Read More »

  • Unravelling the Hydration Barrier of Lignin Oleate Nanoparticles

    Journal: Angewandte Chemie International Edition, 2021

    Abstract: This study investigates the hydration barrier properties of lignin oleate nanoparticles. Advanced characterization techniques reveal the mechanisms by which these nanoparticles resist moisture penetration, highlighting their potential in sustainable packaging and protective coatings. The research provides insights into acid- and base-catalyzed functionalization processes, enhancing the applicability of lignin-based materials in various industrial applications.

    Read More »

  • CB[7]-capped Hybrid AuNPs for PDT/PTT Therapy

    Journal: ACS Applied Polymer Materials, 2020

    Abstract: This paper presents the synthesis of cucurbit[7]uril (CB[7])-capped hybrid conjugated oligomer-gold nanoparticles (AuNPs) designed for photodynamic therapy (PDT) and photothermal therapy (PTT). The nanoparticles exhibit enhanced therapeutic efficacy and biocompatibility, making them promising agents for combined cancer treatment and cellular imaging. The study was featured as a cover article, highlighting its innovative approach and potential impact.

    Read More »

  • Shear-Induced Orientation and Relaxation of Cellulose Nanocrystal and Montmorillonite Nanoplatelet Dispersions

    Journal: Soft Matter, 2022

    Abstract: This study investigates the shear-induced orientation and relaxation behaviors of cellulose nanocrystal (CNC) and montmorillonite nanoplatelet (MMT) dispersions using Rheo-SAXS. The research provides critical insights into the rheological properties and structural dynamics of these nanocomposites under shear stress, informing optimized manufacturing processes for enhanced mechanical and thermal properties.
