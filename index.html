Hierarchical Incorporation of rGO into Anisotropic CNF Foams

Journal: ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces, 2024, 16, 45337.

Abstract: This study explores the hierarchical integration of reduced graphene oxide (rGO) into cellulose nanofiber (CNF) foams to enhance their mechanical and electrical properties. The research demonstrates significant improvements in foam strength and conductivity, making them suitable for advanced applications in electronics and energy storage.

Magnetic Hybrid Foams from Fe3O4@TA and TOCN

Journal: RSC Advances, 2023, 13, 13919.

Abstract: This research introduces magnetic hybrid foams synthesized using Fe3O4@TA and TEMPO-oxidized cellulose nanofibers (TOCN). The resulting foams demonstrate excellent magnetic responsiveness and mechanical strength, suitable for applications in environmental remediation and smart materials.
