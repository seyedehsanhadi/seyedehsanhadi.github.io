Nanocellulose-Reinforced Polymer Composites

This project focuses on the integration of nanocellulose fibers into polymer matrices to create composites with superior mechanical properties. The use of nanocellulose not only enhances strength and durability but also contributes to the sustainability of the materials by utilizing renewable resources.

Key Achievements:

  • Achieved a 30% increase in tensile strength compared to traditional composites.
  • Reduced composite density by 15%, resulting in lightweight materials suitable for automotive applications.
  • Published findings in the Journal of Composite Materials.
